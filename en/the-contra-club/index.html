The Contra Club

The Contra Club is a fiction web series featuring a hybrid art style that combines 2D animation with 3D environments. Through this project, I developed key skills in visual storytelling, three-dimensional environment design, and art direction using Blender. The series has achieved notable reach on platforms like TikTok and YouTube, accumulating over 300,000 combined views.
